Knowledge Base (FAQ)

Frequently asked questions by our clients.

⏰ How often should chemical cleaning of installations be performed? +


For installations in multi-family buildings and hotels, cleaning is recommended every 2–5 years. In facilities with an increased sanitary risk (e.g., hospitals) – even every 12–24 months.

⚡ Does cleaning require shutting down the installation for a long time? +


A typical chemical cleaning process takes from a few hours to one business day. For large installations, the work schedule is agreed upon individually, e.g., over the weekend.

🛡️ Are the agents used safe for the environment? +


Yes, we use only certified, non-corrosive preparations. After the process, we perform full chemical neutralization and thorough rinsing, which guarantees 100% safety.

🦠 Does cleaning eliminate Legionella bacteria? +


Absolutely. Our chemical rinsing of domestic hot water installations effectively removes biofilm, which is the main habitat and breeding ground for Legionella bacteria.

💡 How does cleaning affect bills? +


Boiler scale deposits act as an insulator. Removing them improves heat exchange, which can reduce the energy consumption needed for heating or cooling by up to 15–20%.

📋 Do you issue protocols for the performed works? +


We close every order with an official technical report and a protocol. For institutions, we prepare the required documentation to be submitted to the Sanitary Inspectorate and other control bodies.
