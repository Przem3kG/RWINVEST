Service Offer

We specialize in professional descaling of installations and comprehensive construction services for industry and residential building.

Industrial and Heating Cleaning

Cleaning of heat exchangers

We remove boiler scale deposits from plate, tubular, brazed, and bolted exchangers, restoring their full thermal efficiency.

Cleaning of central heating boilers and installations

Professional chemical cleaning of boilers and entire central heating systems, preventing failures and improving heat flow.

Cleaning of DHW installations

Descaling and disinfection of domestic hot water installations. We remove accumulated deposits and microbiological threats (e.g., Legionella).

Cleaning / pickling of boilers

We carry out pickling processes for water and steam boilers, ensuring the cleanliness of heating surfaces and operational safety.

Cogeneration system equipment

Specialized cleaning of complex systems and equipment operating in modern cogeneration systems.

Pickling and passivation of installations

Comprehensive protection of pipelines and industrial installations, removing rust and creating a protective layer against corrosion.

Cooling System Cleaning

Evaporators and condensers

Cleaning aggregate components of organic and mineral dirt, improving heat exchange and reducing energy consumption.

Cooling towers and condensers

We use specialized preparations that effectively remove deposits, while being completely safe for galvanized coatings.

Dry-Cooler condensers

Chemical washing of delicate fins and internal systems, extending the life of dry coolers.

Chilled water installations

Flushing and descaling of cooling circuits and pipelines supplying industrial and office buildings.

General Construction Works

General construction works from A to Z

Comprehensive execution of construction investments – from site preparation and foundations to delivering the building in a raw or "turnkey" state.

Construction of housing estates

Professional construction of single-family housing estates and multi-family buildings, maintaining the highest execution standards.

Finishing and renovation works

Full range of modernization and finishing works for apartments, houses, and large public and industrial buildings.

Supervision and project management

Expert technical advice and full engineering supervision over the course of construction works at every stage of the investment.

Renovation and repair of balconies

We offer comprehensive renovations and repairs of balconies in multi-family buildings, ensuring safety, durability, and aesthetic finish.

Require an individual approach?

We treat every investment – from cleaning installations to building a house – as a priority. We provide professional diagnosis and a free quote.
