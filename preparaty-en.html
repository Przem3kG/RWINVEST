Professional Chemistry

Advanced preparations for cleaning industrial, heating, and cooling installations.

We use and distribute specialized chemical preparations that guarantee the highest efficiency in dissolving mineral and microbiological deposits, while maintaining the safety of the cleaned installations.

RADINER Fi

Purpose:

Heat exchangers – plate, shell and tube, and other stainless steel devices and installations.

Construction materials:

Chromium-nickel (stainless) steel, copper and its alloys, gray cast iron.

Types of removed deposits:

Calcium and magnesium carbonates (water scale), corrosion products – iron oxides, passivation of stainless steel.

RADINER Fs/Zn

Purpose:

Cooling installations and evaporative condensers made of galvanized steel, cooling towers.

Construction materials:

Cooling installations and evaporative condensers made of galvanized steel, cooling towers.

Types of removed deposits:

Calcium and magnesium carbonates (water scale), corrosion products – iron oxides.

RADINER ST

Purpose:

Heat exchangers, heating and cooling installations, water and steam boilers, cooling systems.

Construction materials:

Chromium-nickel steel (304, 316), carbon steel, copper and its alloys, aluminum.

Types of removed deposits:

Exclusively calcium and magnesium carbonates (water scale).

RADINER KPX-Z

Purpose:

Heat exchangers, central heating and domestic hot water installations, cooling systems, steam generators, steam boilers.

Construction materials:

Chromium-nickel (stainless) steel, carbon steel, cast iron.

Types of removed deposits:

Hard-to-dissolve mineral deposits, e.g., gypsum, phosphates, other sulfates.

RADINER Fi/Ox

Purpose:

Chemical regeneration of brass water meters, heat meters, revitalization.

Construction materials:

Copper and its alloys, brass, also non-corrosive to carbon steel.

Types of removed deposits:

Calcium and magnesium carbonate (water scale), iron and copper corrosion products.

RADINER Fi/Al

Purpose:

Aluminum elements – radiators, coolers, etc., water installations.

Construction materials:

Aluminum, chromium-nickel steel, carbon steel, copper and its alloys.

Types of removed deposits:

Calcium and magnesium carbonates (water scale), corrosion products – iron oxides (at elevated temps).

RADINER Fs

Purpose:

Water and steam boilers, central heating installations, carbon steel and cast iron installations, iron removers, manganese removers.

Construction materials:

Carbon steel, cast iron, copper and its alloys, concrete, brick.

Types of removed deposits:

Calcium and magnesium carbonates (water scale), corrosion products (iron oxides).

RENSTUD JM

Purpose:

Membrane disinfection processes.

Action:

Removal of microbiological deposits.
